Services Built Around Your Property

Choose a category to see how IronLeaf helps homeowners build healthier lawns, cleaner landscapes, and outdoor spaces that hold their look over time.

Mulch & Rock

Refresh tired beds, add clean contrast, and give planting areas a finished look that frames the home.

Best For

Faded beds, bare soil, rock upgrades, and yards that need stronger definition from the street.

Includes

Bed preparation, mulch installation or refresh, decorative stone, river rock, clean edging, and final cleanup.

Result

Defined beds, richer contrast, and a cleaner frame around your landscape.

Lawn & Turf

Create a healthier, smoother lawn with the preparation and care needed for lasting curb appeal.

Best For

Bare areas, failing turf, uneven lawns, patch repairs, and yards ready for a cleaner reset.

Includes

Preparation, turf removal when needed, grading, sod placement, aeration, treatments, and cleanup.

Result

A greener, more even lawn that improves the way the whole property presents.

Beds & Planting

Shape the spaces around your home with cleaner bed lines, balanced plantings, and color that feels intentional.

Best For

New beds, outdated planting areas, curb appeal upgrades, seasonal color, and landscape refreshes.

Includes

Bed layout, edging, renovation, shrub and flower installation, removals, and cleanup.

Result

Defined planting areas that make the home feel cared for and complete.

Cleanups & Trimmings

Reset overgrown or debris-filled areas so the property feels orderly, maintained, and ready to enjoy.

Best For

Seasonal overgrowth, listing preparation, event prep, and neglected outdoor spaces.

Includes

Seasonal cleanup, leaf removal, storm cleanup, junk removal, hedge trimming, and bush trimming.

Result

A sharper exterior that feels clean, controlled, and ready to be seen.

Christmas Lights

Bring the season to life with clean rooflines, thoughtful placement, and a holiday display that looks organized from every angle.

Best For

Rooflines, trees, shrubs, walkways, entryways, and homeowners who want the holiday look without the stress.

Includes

Display planning, secure installation, clean placement, adjustments, takedown, and cleanup.

Result

A warm, polished Christmas display that feels festive, balanced, and ready for the season.

Tree Services

Open up the yard, improve clearance, and remove unwanted tree problems with clean, careful work.

Best For

Overgrown trees, low limbs, unwanted trees, stumps, and outdoor areas that need better clearance.

Includes

Tree trimming, shaping, removal, stump grinding, branch cleanup, and debris hauling.

Result

A safer, cleaner property with better visibility and more usable outdoor space.

Exterior Cleaning

Restore the first impression of your home with cleaner siding, brighter surfaces, and careful washing methods.

Best For

Dirty siding, stained concrete, algae, mildew, brick buildup, patios, windows, and walkways.

Includes

House washing, window cleaning, concrete cleaning, brick cleaning, patio cleaning, rinsing, and final check.

Result

A brighter exterior with stronger curb appeal and a cleaner welcome home.

Hardscaping & Outdoor Living

Add structure, flow, and everyday function to the outdoor areas you want to use more often.

Best For

Pavers, walkways, retaining walls, fire pits, gathering areas, and outdoor spaces that need a built-in finish.

Includes

Planning, layout, material coordination, site preparation, installation, detailing, and cleanup.

Result

A more useful outdoor space with better flow, stronger structure, and lasting appeal.
